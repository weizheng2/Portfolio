Final Kick is a mobile football penalty game.
This game was made years ago with Unity script, but Google Play required to update certain plugins and packages for it to keep working, so after the Studio upgraded the Unity engine version I was tasked with reimplementing all previous sdks and plugins, and also adding new ones. Fix bugs that appeared after upgrading the engine, and new plugins. Also making new builds for Google Play and App Store.
Below are some of my main contributions:

    General

  • Implement UI screens structure and functionality based on the artist designs, taking into account different resolutions.
  • Ensure any feature or update can be added without disrupting the previous versions. Allowing the coexistence of multiples versions from the same store or different stores.
  • Implemented different A/B testing processes, where the game showed different features or with some variations to test which one performed better.

    Village Mode

  • A village mode, where players can collect and upgrade buildings that provide different boosts for their account.

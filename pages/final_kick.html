Final Kick

Dark Curry


  • Type: Professional Work
  • Links: Google Play, App Store
  • Role: Unity Programmer
  • Studio: Dark Curry (Spain)
  • Time: September 2023 - December 2023

Final Kick is a mobile football penalty game originally developed with JavaScript (UnityScript).
With Google Play requiring updates to various plugins and packages, the studio upgraded the Unity engine, now using C#.
After that, I was asigned to re-implement previous SDK and plugins while integrating new ones. Additionally, I addressed emerging bugs resulting from the engine upgrade. I also handled the process of making new builds for both Google Play and App Store.
Below are some of my main contributions:

    General

  • Compile builds and submit the game for Google Play Store and Apple App Store, ensuring compliance with their requirements.
  • Fix bugs from the engine update, and occasional bugs reported by the players.
  • Update and implement new plugins and SDK.

    SDK and Plugins Examples

  • Anzu Ads SDK example, for in game fence ads.
  • Yodo Ads SDK example, for interstitial and banner ads.
  • Most of the plugins updated required a complete new implementation since the majority of the content was deprecated.
